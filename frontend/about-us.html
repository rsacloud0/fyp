How It Works

A session is built to help you move somewhere, not just ask randomly.

1

Define the aim

Pick a goal like understanding a topic, evaluating an argument, or making a decision with more confidence.

2

Think out loud

Ask questions, test assumptions, and let the model adapt its responses to the way you are currently reasoning.

3

Get challenged

Receive a sharper question, a framework, or a nudge to move up a Bloom level when your thinking is ready for it.

4

Review the session

Come back to your chats, concepts, resources, and metrics later so the session still has value after the conversation ends.

Bloom's Taxonomy

A simple path from recall to creation.

1 Remember Recall facts
2 Understand Explain ideas
3 Apply Use in context
4 Analyse Break apart
5 Evaluate Judge quality
6 Create Build new ideas
